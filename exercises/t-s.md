# Coarticulation: /t/ + /s/

## Sound Combination

**First sound:** /t/ — voiceless alveolar stop
**Second sound:** /s/ — voiceless alveolar fricative

---

## Practice Exercises

### Example 1
**Phrase:** at school
**IPA:** `/æ(t̚) skuɫ/`

**Notes:** Homorganic alveolar: /t/ often deleted before /s/; or unreleased /t̚s/

---

### Example 2
**Phrase:** cats
**IPA:** `/kʰæts/`

**Notes:** Released /t/ within word: audible [ts] sequence

---

### Example 3
**Phrase:** it seems
**IPA:** `/ɪt̚ simz/`

**Notes:** /t/ may be deleted or unreleased before /s/
