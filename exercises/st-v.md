## 2. Coarticulatory Mechanisms

1. **Anticipatory Place Shift:**
   The tongue/lips begin moving toward the labiodental target before the /st/ gesture completes. This creates overlap between alveolar and labiodental articulations.

2. **Voicing Transition:**
   The glottis shifts from voiceless to voiced. This transition may show partial devoicing or voicing anticipation depending on speech rate.

3. **Stop Release Modification:**
   The /st/ closure releases directly into the /v/ articulation, often showing reduced or absent burst energy.

4. **Specific Adaptation:**
   Voiceless ‚Üí voiced fricative
