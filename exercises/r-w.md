## 2. Coarticulatory Mechanisms

1. **Anticipatory Place Shift:**
   The tongue/lips begin moving toward the labial-velar target before the /r/ gesture completes. This creates overlap between alveolar/postalveolar and labial-velar articulations.

2. **Voicing Continuity:**
   Both /r/ and /w/ are voiced; the glottis remains adducted throughout. Spectrographically, this appears as a continuous low-frequency voicing bar.

3. **Specific Adaptation:**
   Rhotic ‚Üí labiovelar
