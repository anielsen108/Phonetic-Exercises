# Coarticulation: /v/ + /t/

## Sound Combination

**First sound:** /v/ — voiced labiodental fricative
**Second sound:** /t/ — voiceless alveolar stop

## Practice Exercises

**IPA:** `/hæv tə/`

**Notes:** Voiced fricative → voiceless stop: /t/ may devoice /v/ slightly
