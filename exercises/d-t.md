# Coarticulation: /d/ + /t/

## Sound Combination

**First sound:** /d/ — voiced alveolar stop
**Second sound:** /t/ — voiceless alveolar stop

## Practice Exercises

### Example 1
**Phrase:** bad time
**IPA:** `/bæd̥̚ tʰaɪm/`

**Notes:** Devoicing of /d/ before /t/: often merges to [t̚]

### Example 2
**Phrase:** need to
**IPA:** `/nid̚ tə/`

**Notes:** May reduce to single [t̚] or remain [d̚t]
