# Coarticulation: /d/ + /m/

## Sound Combination

**First sound:** /d/ — voiced alveolar stop
**Second sound:** /m/ — bilabial nasal

## Practice Exercises

### Example 1
**Phrase:** bad man
**IPA:** `/bæd̚ mæn/`

**Notes:** Nasal release possible: velum lowers during /m/
