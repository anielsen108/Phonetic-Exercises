## 2. Coarticulatory Mechanisms

1. **Anticipatory Place Shift:**
   The tongue/lips begin moving toward the velar target before the /h/ gesture completes. This creates overlap between glottal and velar articulations.

2. **Fricative-to-Stop Transition:**
   The continuous airflow of /h/ terminates as the /k/ closure forms. Listeners perceive a sharp acoustic boundary.

3. **Specific Adaptation:**
