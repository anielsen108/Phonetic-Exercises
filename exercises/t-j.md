1. **Anticipatory Place Shift:**
   The tongue/lips begin moving toward the palatal target before the /t/ gesture completes. This creates overlap between alveolar and palatal articulations.

3. **Stop Release Modification:**
   The /t/ closure releases directly into the /j/ articulation, often showing reduced or absent burst energy.
