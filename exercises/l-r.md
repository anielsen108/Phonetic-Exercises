## 2. Coarticulatory Mechanisms

1. **Anticipatory Place Shift:**
   The tongue/lips begin moving toward the alveolar/postalveolar target before the /l/ gesture completes. This creates overlap between alveolar and alveolar/postalveolar articulations.

2. **Voicing Continuity:**
   Both /l/ and /r/ are voiced; the glottis remains adducted throughout. Spectrographically, this appears as a continuous low-frequency voicing bar.

3. **Specific Adaptation:**
   Lateral ‚Üí rhotic
